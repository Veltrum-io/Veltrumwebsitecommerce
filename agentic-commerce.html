What's inside

One platform that runs
every commerce workflow.

Six product surfaces, one data model, one set of agents. Plug in your stack and Veltrum becomes the operating layer your team uses every day.

Storefront orchestration

Catalog, pricing, inventory and promotions across Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento and your custom storefronts — kept in lock-step in real time.

Order & payment flow

Ingest orders from every channel, normalise, dedupe, and run them through pre-shipment confirmation, fraud checks and split tender — before the warehouse picks.

Logistics & carrier mix

Delhivery, Bluedart, Shiprocket, Shadowfax, Xpressbees — Veltrum picks the right carrier per shipment, watches RTO, and resolves NDRs without a human ticket.

Marketing performance

Live ROAS by channel, by SKU, by cohort. Veltrum pauses creatives that stop converting and reallocates spend across Meta, Google and influencer in the same loop.

Customer experience

Voice agents on calls, AI on WhatsApp, full audit trail in your CRM. WISMO, returns, refund-status, escalations — all auto-resolved or routed in one flow.

Finance & reconciliation

Razorpay, Cashfree, Tally and your bank feeds — reconciled to the rupee. Carrier invoice disputes raised, settled and posted automatically.
