Marketplace Intelligence

One council. Every marketplace you sell on.

Veltrum plugs into the marketplaces where your brand sells and turns your own store data into a nightly action plan — the same council process that runs across Shopify, Meta, and WhatsApp. Live today on Amazon, with more marketplaces on the roadmap.

Live today · Amazon Selling Partner API

Order, inventory & margin intelligence — built entirely from your own Amazon store.

Veltrum connects to your Amazon Seller Central account through the Selling Partner API and reads your own orders, inventory, and settlement data. Specialist agents turn that data into a nightly action plan, the same way they do for Shopify — reorder alerts, margin breakdowns, and sales trend reporting, delivered every morning.

Order & inventory sync

Zoro · Inventory

Live sync of orders, stock levels, and returns from your Amazon account. Reorder alerts before you stock out, and dead-inventory flags before capital gets stuck.

Margin & fee breakdown

Roy · Finance

Reconciles referral fees, FBA charges, and ad spend against revenue to show true per-SKU margin — not just top-line sales.

Sales trend reporting

Senku · Business Analytics

Tracks day-over-day sales, conversion, and category trend signals for your own catalog — benchmarked against your own history, not other sellers.

All analytics above are generated exclusively from your own Amazon seller account data. Veltrum does not aggregate, benchmark, or share data across different sellers' accounts.
