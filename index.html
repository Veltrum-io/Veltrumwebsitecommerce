What is Veltrum?

Most AI is a tool.
You use it
when you remember to.

Veltrum is a team.
They work
while you sleep.

Your business runs across Shopify, Meta, WhatsApp, your warehouse, your finance stack, and your logistics partner. Each platform knows its slice. None of them talk to each other. Every decision you make is missing context.

Veltrum builds a shared brain across all of it. 9 specialist agents — each owning a domain — read their data independently, then deliberate in a structured 3-round council. They argue. They resolve. They produce a joint action plan.

You wake up to a brief at 7AM. One tap to approve each action. Nothing executes without you — but everything is ready to go the moment you say yes.

Built for D2C brands on Shopify doing ₹10L–₹5Cr monthly GMV.

How it works

From integration to morning brief — in three steps.

01
Connect once

Your stack plugs in

Shopify, Meta Ads, WhatsApp, Razorpay, Delhivery, Amazon — 10+ live integrations feed real-time data into a shared brand context. One setup, continuous sync.

02
Every night at 3AM

9 agents deliberate

Each agent reads its domain independently. Then they meet in a structured council: deliberate, challenge, decide. Conflicts are surfaced before they cost you money.

03
7AM morning brief

One plan, your approval

You wake up to a council report. What happened, what was decided, what needs you. Every customer-facing action is held until you approve. You stay in control.

The council in action

Real-time tactical council

No human team produces this reasoning in 28 seconds for every abandoned cart. No single AI model can — it only holds one slice of data.

Veltrum's agents each hold a different context. In a structured deliberation, the output is better than any one of them could produce alone. Roy knows your margins. Holo knows the customer. Miku knows what converts.

The blocking mechanic: if Jiraiya flags an open dispute, Hinata's win-back is blocked until it resolves. Real business logic — without anyone programming it.
